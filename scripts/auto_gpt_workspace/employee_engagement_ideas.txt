Here are some ideas to foster professional development in the workplace:

1. Offer on-site training
2. Create peer learning opportunities
3. Enable job shadowing
4. Organize mentorship programs
5. Support attendance at conferences and workshops
6. Encourage reading and online learning platforms
7. Offer tuition assistance or reimbursement programs
8. Encourage cross-team collaborations
9. Foster candid feedback and evaluation
10. Provide diverse job opportunities and career paths
